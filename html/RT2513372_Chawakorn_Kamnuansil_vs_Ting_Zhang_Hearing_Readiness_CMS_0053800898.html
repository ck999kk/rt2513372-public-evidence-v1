Good morning parties,

 

Your case is proceeding to a hearing. Shortly, VCAT will schedule a date and time for your case, but first there is some information we need from you to prepare for hearing. 

Please see below for the information and documentation required for each party, and the dates this information must be submitted. Documents must be provided to VCAT, via return email, and to the other party/parties or upload directly in the portal, and send to the other party/parties if they are not active in the portal. 

Documents must be submitted as separate files, saved with a name that describes what they are (for example ‘Exit Condition Report’). Zip files and drop box links will not be accepted.  

Renter  

Chawakorn Kamnuansil: Due by Monday 16th February 2026

 Provide the below: 

  • a completed Summary of Proofs – Renter’s Compensation form
  • proof of having sent the application to the other party/parties (if not already provided)
  • the following documents:
  • Residential Rental Agreement
  • If claiming bond
  • Residential Tenancies Bond Authority (RTBA) bond receipt
  • If claiming arrears or breach of fixed terms lease:
  • Rental ledger
  • Evidence of Renter notice of intention to vacate or Provider Notice to vacate
  • If claiming damage and/or cleaning:
  • Entry and exit condition reports
  • Invoice/s
  • Breach notice/s
  • Any relevant evidence to the claim
  • Required to pay the application fees, please refer to the portal to make the payment.
  • Indicate any unavailable dates starting the week of 25th May 2026. 

Note: If you do not comply with the above, the application may be struck out without a hearing. 

 

Rental Provider  

Ting Zhang: Once you receive the above documents, you are required to provide the below information to the other party/parties and VCAT, via return email, by Monday 23rd February 2026. 

Provide the below: 

  • Any written response that you wish to make to the renter’s claim, setting out the parts you agree or disagree with, and why
  • All documents you wish to rely on in response to the claim
  • Indicate any unavailable dates starting the week of 25th May 2026. 

Note: If you do not comply with the above, VCAT may proceed to deal with this case based on the documents and other evidence available to VCAT at the hearing, and may make an order against you.  

If you have any questions, please contact me on 1300 01 7378 or email at cases@courts.vic.gov.au.
