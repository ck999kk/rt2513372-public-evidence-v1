Thank you for your email. I understand your application relates to urgent repairs and compensation.

 

The urgent repair component must be dealt with first as it is considered more urgent. If this matter progressed to a VCAT hearing only the urgent repairs would be considered in a first hearing.

 

As a resolution appears to have been reached regarding the repairs component I will proceed to strike this matter out with a right of reinstatement. 

 

What this means is if you wish to proceed with a compensation claim this would be dealt with at a later date. To do this you would request to reinstate your case using the attached form if the repairs were not completed as agreed or if you wish to pursue compensation once the repairs are completed.

 

If you wish to obtain legal advice or information you may wish to reach out to Tenants Victoria  https://tenantsvic.org.au/

 

Please let me know if you have any questions.
