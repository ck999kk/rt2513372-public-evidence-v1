My name is Stacey, and I am a Resolution Coordinator with the Resolution Team at RDRV. Our role is to support parties in resolving matters as early as possible, often without the need for a hearing.

You have recently lodged an application regarding unit 1803/243 Franklin St, Melbourne, VIC, 3004, and I will be assisting you with this matter from start to finish.

 

To help us progress your application efficiently, I would appreciate your responses to the following questions:

  • What outcome are you hoping to achieve through this application?
  • Have you provided a copy of the application to the other party?

    • If yes, please provide a brief summary.
  • Are you open to discussing a resolution before the hearing?
  • Is there any counter claim application made by the respondent?
  • What suburb or country town do you reside in?

If applicable, please also provide:

  • Invoices or documentation to support any bond or compensation claims;

  • Any missing details in the application (e.g. bond number, claim amounts, respondent’s contact information);
